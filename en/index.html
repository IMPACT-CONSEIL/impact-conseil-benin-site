The two-way consulting firm between West Africa and Europe

We support businesses, NGOs and institutions in Benin and WAEMU, and we connect both shores: Europe to West Africa, West Africa to Europe.

What we do

Impact Conseil is a management and sustainability consulting firm based in Cotonou, Benin, established in 2019 and backed by the European network GCL Experts Gestion. We serve businesses, NGOs and institutions across Benin and the WAEMU region through five integrated practices:

  • Studies, strategy & development: market studies, sector analysis, bankable business plans, expansion strategy
  • Business management (GCL core methodology): cash flow, profitability, 360° audits, turnaround, business transfer
  • Project management: PMO, transition management, change management, socio-economic impact studies
  • Sustainable performance: CSR as a business lever, ISO 9001/14001/22000/45001/50001, HACCP, organic and fair-trade certifications, ESG reporting
  • Human capital & compliance: recruitment, payroll, social and business law (OHADA), training, executive coaching

We also support public policy work: sector policy design, programme evaluation, impact and feasibility studies, value-chain structuring (cotton, cashew, soy, pineapple) and institutional capacity building, for ministries, NGOs and development partners.

The Africa ↔ Europe bridge

Our signature: we work in both directions.

  • Europe → West Africa: feasibility studies, subsidiary setup, local recruitment, compliance and ongoing administration for European companies entering Benin and WAEMU. Local representation available without incorporation.
  • West Africa → Europe: EU compliance, export certifications (HACCP, ISO 22000, organic, fair trade), market access, buyer matchmaking and commercial representation in France through our Rennes office.
Why it matters: no other firm in the region combines the full management consulting range, business-driven CSR, human capital services and a structured partnership with a European network.
